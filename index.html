Behind the Book

This story was born from the belief that love is limitless and layered. It’s not about perfection—it’s about connection. We’ve poured our hearts into these pages hoping it reflects what love can truly look like when you let it grow on its own terms.

About the Authors

D.C.W. = Daniel, Caleb, and Will have been building a life together for nearly a decade.

As a real-life throuple, they’ve shared everything from wild travel adventures and cozy Sunday breakfasts to owning a business, raising two lovable dogs, and maybe even cohabiting with a ghost (their house definitely has vibes).

Together, they created Tres Amigos, Una Vida to celebrate the ups, downs, and deep connection found in loving more than one person at a time. They hope their stories inspire others to embrace their truth, redefine relationships on their own terms, and never settle for anything less than magic.

When they're not writing, you can find them working in their garden, planning their future in Hawaii, or answering questions on their blog, 3Dudes1Life.
